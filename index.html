Frequently Asked Questions

What is a digital footprint?
Your digital footprint is the collection of data that exists about you across public and semi-public sources online. We map and interpret this presence to establish clear situational awareness of your external exposure.
Why is it important to reduce our digital footprint?
Reducing our digital footprint helps maintain privacy, limit unnecessary exposure, and ensure greater control over your personal information in the digital space. When information is widely available, threat actors can analyze and correlate for social engineering and impersonation attempts.
Why choose Thane Security?
We incorporate SLADE, our intelligence framework, which is designed to evaluate digital exposure through human-led analysis of publicly available information by replicating real-world reconnaissance methods used by real-world adversaries. We generate a security posture report including client education on managing the risk.
What information do you need to start?
We typically begin with minimal identifiers such as an email, username, or phone number. From there, we expand using open-source intelligence methods across publicly available data sources.
Is my information kept secure?
Yes. All client-provided data is handled under strict confidentiality protocols and is permanently removed upon completion of the engagement.
Can I remove my data from the web?
We do not directly remove data from third-party platforms. Our reports include an "Education" component to guide you through the process of data deletion and opt-outs.
What is the pricing structure?
Engagements are scoped based on depth of analysis and investigative requirements. Each assessment is tailored to the level of visibility required.
Is this a recurring service?
This is a point-in-time digital exposure audit. Ongoing monitoring and periodic re-assessments are recommended for visibility and managing associated risks.
